Currently, I am Associate Professor at Beihang University (China), School of Mathematical Science. Before that I was working at Texas A&M (USA) with Professor Anne Shiu. I also had Postdoc experiences at University of Bremen (Germany) and National Institute of Mathematical Science (South Korea). I got my PhD at Peking University (China) in 2014. My research interests include computational algebraic geometry, mathematical biology and algebraic statistics.
